Revenue Plan Sensitivity Model

A revenue planning tool for B2B SaaS teams. Enter last year's actuals and this year's target to pressure-test whether your plan is achievable. The model derives implied efficiency metrics from your actuals — close rate, pipeline ROI — and projects forward based on your assumptions. It does not model churn, NRR, CAC, MQL/SQL funnel stages, sales headcount capacity, or revenue recognition timing. Use it to identify your binding constraint and stress-test the levers that actually move the number.
